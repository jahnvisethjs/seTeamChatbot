### WEEK 2 STRUCTURE (Days 6-10)
Each day typically includes:
- **Check-in with supervisor** (15 min) - Address immediate questions/challenges, daily priorities
- **Independent Work / Unstructured Time** (1-2 hrs) - Complete checklist items, explore tools
- **Proxy Activities based on feedback** (1.5-2 hrs) - Use Proxy AI and write improvements and general thoughts
- **Complete end-of-day reflection questions** (30 min)
- **Checkout meeting/reflections** (30 min) - Share thoughts, feelings, questions

Key activities in Week 2:
- Continue Proxy Activities with iterative feedback
- Use Proxy AI and write improvements
- Data curation for Collaboratory Activities
- Review SP25_ProxyAI_Response_Evaluation documents
- Review SP25_GeneralProxying_Response_Evaluation documents
- Independent work on checklist items
- Weekly Collaboratory Standup meeting

### WEEK 3 STRUCTURE (Days 11-15)
Each day continues the pattern but shifts focus:
- More independent work and proxy activities
- Begin contributing to data curation for Collaboratory
- Continue using Proxy AI tools with increasing independence
- Intro to first project: Test Cases Documentation
- Working on real project tasks
- Weekly Collaboratory Standup meeting
- Regular check-ins and reflections continue

### WEEK 4 STRUCTURE (Days 16-21)
Focus shifts to more autonomous work:
- **Independent Work/Unstructured Time** becomes primary
- Work on the test cases document
- Proxy activities based on accumulated feedback
- Continue data curation contributions
- Check-in meetings continue but with increasing autonomy
- Final reflections on the onboarding period
- **30-day end-of-onboarding reflection** at the very end

## RECURRING ACTIVITIES (appear regularly throughout the 21 days)

1. **Daily Check-in with Supervisor** (15 min at start of day)
   - Purpose: Address immediate questions or challenges; provide clarity on daily priorities and tasks; build connection and alignment on key objectives for the day.

2. **Independent Work - Unstructured Time** (varies)
   - Purpose: Use this time block to complete all of the Independent work Checklist items at the top of this document.

3. **Independent Work - Structured Time** (varies)
   - Purpose: Focus on getting to know Collaboratory by viewing pre-recorded resources.

4. **Weekly Collaboratory Standup Meeting** (~1 hr, usually Wednesday)
   - Purpose: To connect with Salvador and Kristin regarding the deliverables, maintain alignment on shared goals, provide quick updates on progress, surface any blockers or needs, and coordinate next steps to keep our work moving forward smoothly and collaboratively.

5. **Proxy Activities** (1-2 hrs)
   - Purpose: Work on proxy activities provided by Pavan; seek necessary clarifications. Use Proxy AI and write improvements and general thoughts. Contribute to data curation for Collaboratory Activities through proxying and reviewing the performance of Proxy AI.

6. **Complete end-of-day reflection questions** (30 min)
   - Purpose: Space to reflect and share thoughts, fostering ongoing communication and improvement. Use the onboarding reflection questions document.

7. **Checkout meeting/reflections of the day** (30 min at end of day)
   - Purpose: Your thoughts, feelings, and questions matter here.

8. **Break** (15-30 min, mid-afternoon)
